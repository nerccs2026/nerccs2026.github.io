\section{For Remote Presenters}

\begin{itemize}
    \item Check the program book for your \textbf{room assignment} and \textbf{talk time (EDT)}.
    \item \textbf{Talk slots are 20 minutes} (15 min talk + 5 min Q\&A).
    \item Join your assigned Zoom room \textbf{at least 10 minutes before your session begins} so the host can promote you to presenter and test your screen share.
    \item You will share your screen directly in Zoom. Test your screen-share before the conference.
    \item Contact \href{mailto:nerccs2026@gmail.com}{nerccs2026@gmail.com} in advance if you have any AV concerns.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Pre-Talk Checklist}
\begin{itemize}
    \item[$\square$] Stable internet connection confirmed
    \item[$\square$] Zoom desktop client installed and updated (not browser-only)
    \item[$\square$] Microphone and camera tested
    \item[$\square$] Slides ready to screen-share
    \item[$\square$] Joined the correct room and promoted to presenter by host
    \item[$\square$] Notified session chair of any special requirements
\end{itemize}
